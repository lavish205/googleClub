KeyNotes

Objective
To form a team of students in campus who will be taking care of all the Google related issues and organize related events for betterment of students.
Benefits of Google Student Club
  • Students will get an environment of applied learning and have better awareness for all kinds of technical and non-technical events on and off-campus.
  • Students will have the awareness of various programs of Google such as Scholarships for women and Internship in Google.
  • There is no such community in college to whom students can go and ask for their career related or technical queries. Hence GSC would act as the community who would help students for the same.
  • Students can get placed in Google with consistent working. They lack awareness of how they can be placed. Club will help themselves and students to get placed in Google.
  • Students will learn judicious use of technology and Google apps for education that actually make learning very easy.
  • Students will come together to take part in Coding Challenges too. Presently there is no coding culture in college. We mostly code in labs, and thatâ€™s it. Club would help in promoting coding as well as app development skills in students.
  • Google Goodies/Swags and Certificates to best performers.
  • There are many other benefits of having a club of students in campus who with support of college authority can make the college a better learning place. Students will come up with new achievements, mobile applications, websites of their own.
Walk Through
  • We will firstly form a team of enthusiastic students. Then we will train other students in field of Android, Cloud, Development, Internet etc.
  • We will allow every student to come up to us for their queries related to careers or even coding/technical queries and solve those problem with us.
  • With a good student support base we will organize techincal and non-technical workshops
Selection Procedure
To become member of Google Student Club you can apply here or visit Become Member page. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted via preferred choice of contact. Then they will have further tasks to prove their interest in club. This is going to be big. Be a part of the revolution.
