KeyNotes

Do Cool Things That Matter.

Objective:

To form a team of students from within the campus with the purpose of organizing skill based Google related events leading to technical upliftment of students on campus and beyond.

Benefits of Google Student Club (our unique and exclusive features):

  • Provide a platform for applied learning and rational thought develpoment.
  • Generate awareness about plethora of technical and non-technical happenings across the nation.
  • Keep students updated about various scholarship and internship opportunities offered by Google.
  • Provide technical career guidance to students and solve queries regarding the same.
  • Help students gain Google certification through various Google Education Programs.
  • Provide ground for intensive and vivid coding and prepare students for competitive coding challenges.
  • Create outreach programs for students to benefit from other GSCs across Jaipur.
  • Stand a chance to win Goggle swags, goodies, certificates and sponsored trips.
  • Encourage students to come up with noval ideas for community problems via app development, web development, marketing solutions, design thinking, prototyping business models and beyond.

Walk Through:

  • A team of higly enthusiastic individuals will be formed. They will help train other students in the field of Android, Cloud, Digital Marketing, Internet of Things and the likes.
  • All students are welcome to share any technical difficulty they face academically or otherwise. An open, community driven approch shall be employed to solve any grievances thereof.
  • Banking on a positive reponse from students and faculty of our intitution, we shall be able to organize high end workshops and talks on campus.

Selection Procedure:

To become member of the Google Student Club you can apply here or visit Become Member page. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted via preferred choice of contact.Their task at hand will then be prove worthy of their selection.
