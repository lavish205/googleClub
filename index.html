Webathon -Introduction to Web

Summary:

Event is meant to educated students of the vast variety of opportunities that are available on the internet with a series of presentation on basic web technologies that thrive the net like: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Front-End Frameworks. This will provide a basic idea to students who are not updated with the latest trends and making sure that everyone is on the same platform of understanding for moving ahead into Cloud and Android technologies.

Targeted Audience:

  • Students of 2nd and Pre-Final Year
  • Branches: Computer Science & Electronics and Communication
